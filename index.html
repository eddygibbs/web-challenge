The Rise to the Web Challenge is to be Fronted by The Presidents Forum For Associations (PFA) but under the guidance of Students Information and Computing Association (SICA). This challenge spans over a duration of approximately 6-8 weeks and involves a collaborative effort between Associations and skilled Developers to create bespoke 5STAR websites. The challenge is divided into key phases designed to facilitate seamless website development, encourage collaboration, and showcase digital innovation. The Phases include;
